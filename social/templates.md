# Reusable Templates — Captions, Hooks, DMs

---

## Caption Formula

Every caption follows this structure:

```
[One-line hook — what they just saw]

[2–3 lines expanding on the result or value]

[One line: who this is for]

DM [KEYWORD] [call to action phrase].
```

Keep it short. No paragraphs. Use line breaks aggressively.

---

## Hook Templates

Fill in the brackets for any project:

**Result-first hooks**:
- "We built [CLIENT] a [PRODUCT] for $[PRICE]"
- "This [TYPE OF BUSINESS] just got [FEATURE/UPGRADE]"
- "[TYPE OF BUSINESS] + [TECH] = [RESULT]"
- "Every [TYPE OF BUSINESS] needs [FEATURE]"

**Curiosity hooks**:
- "Most [type of business] don't have this. [Client] does."
- "Here's what [price] gets you when you work with us"
- "We built [X] in [timeframe]. Here's what it looks like."

**Problem-first hooks**:
- "[Pain point]. We fixed it for [client]."
- "DoorDash takes 25%. This takes zero."
- "Missed calls = lost money. We fixed that for [client]."

## Story / Teaser Templates (for series content)

Use these when a project isn't finished yet — builds follow momentum.

**Teaser**:
```
Building something for [client].

It's not done yet. Follow to watch it go live.

[One line on what it'll do when finished]
```

**Update**:
```
[Client] update — Week [X]

Done: [✓ list]
In progress: [list]
Waiting on: [client blockers]

[CTA or hook for next update]
```

**Reveal**:
```
[Client] is live.

Here's everything we built:
— [feature 1]
— [feature 2]
— [feature 3]

Took [X weeks]. Cost $[price].

DM [KEYWORD] to get something like this.
```

## Engagement Responses

When people comment on your videos, respond quickly with something short.
Do NOT post your full pitch in comments — take it to DMs.

**Positive comment**:
"Thanks 🙏 — DM me [KEYWORD] and I'll send details"

**"How much?"**:
"DM me QUOTE — I'll put a full scope together for you in 24hrs"

**"Do you do [X]?"**:
"Yeah we do — DM me and I'll walk you through it"

**Competitor comment / "I can do this cheaper"**:
Just ignore. Don't engage.

**Other business owner tagging someone**:
Reply to the tag: "Hey [name] — DM me WEBSITE if you want to see what we 
built for [client]"
