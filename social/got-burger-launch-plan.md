## Pre-Launch Content (while waiting on client)

These posts build anticipation without needing anything from the client.

### "We built 80% before getting paid" (Post now)
Already in got-burger-scripts.md. Post this while waiting on the deposit.

### "Waiting on the client" story (authentic content)
```
Got Burger update — still waiting on:
☐ Final menu items + prices from the owner
☐ Logo file
☐ Domain access to connect to Vercel
☐ POS decision (Square or new account)
☐ $1,625 deposit 🤞

Just need the green light.

```

This is authentic. People love watching real projects with real blockers.
It humanizes the agency and makes people invested in the outcome.

### "Here's what we're waiting on and why it matters" post
Explain why you need the logo, domain, etc.
Educates future clients on what YOU need from THEM to move fast.
Also positions you as organized and professional.

## Launch Day Content Plan

When the deposit lands and you get domain access — document everything.

**The day you connect the domain**:
Story: "Got Burger domain just connected. Goes live in minutes."
Reel: Screen recording of adding the domain in Vercel dashboard.
Caption: "This is what deploying a restaurant website looks like."

**The moment it goes live**:
Story: "gotburgerrestaurant.com is live 🍔"
Reel: Open a new incognito browser → type the URL → watch the site load → show the 3D burger → scroll through everything.
Caption: "It's live. Got Burger, Ypsilanti MI. Full website + AI caller coming soon."

**The 3D reveal on launch day**:
Your best video. Film it specifically for this moment.
Start with a blank browser tab → type gotburgerrestaurant.com → watch it load → 3D burger spins → hold for 10 seconds.
Add the domain URL as text overlay. Show it's real and live.

**The launch Reel**:
Full compilation. 30 seconds max.
Homepage → menu → cart → checkout → location → digital display.
Quick cuts. Music drops hard on the 3D burger reveal.
Text: "Got Burger is live. Ypsilanti, MI."
End: "$1,625 deal. DM WEBSITE to get yours."
