# Instagram Stories — Daily Content Ideas

Stories are where you stay top-of-mind between Reels.
Post 3–5 stories a day. Quick, raw, behind-the-scenes.
No editing required. Just phone camera.

---

## Story Formats

### Daily "What I'm building" story
Post at the start of your workday. One screen or desk photo.
Text: "Working on [X] today"
This takes 10 seconds and keeps you in the algorithm daily.

### "This is what [thing] looks like" story
Quick screen recording or photo of something in your work.
Examples:
- The Got Burger website open on your monitor
- The finance automation running in terminal
- The Excel sheet auto-populating
- A Pushover notification popping up
- The AI dashboard
No caption needed. Let the visual speak.

### Price reveal story
Just text on a black background. No image.
"Restaurant website with AI phone caller: $1,625 upfront + $400/month"
"What would you pay for this for your restaurant? Vote below"
→ Poll: "Too expensive" / "That's a deal"

### Behind the scenes story (3-slide sequence)
Slide 1: "Here's what we're building this week" (text)
Slide 2: Screenshot of code or design
Slide 3: "This goes live soon. Follow to see it." + Follow CTA sticker

### "Before vs After" story (2 slides)
Slide 1: "Before" — show the problem (restaurant with no website, busy phone, bad UX)
Slide 2: "After" — show your solution
Use the "Previous" / "Now" stickers

### Ask Me Anything (every Friday)
"Ask me anything about websites / AI / building a business"
Reply to every response. DMs people who reply.
Turns into sales conversations naturally.

### Client update story
"Got Burger update — waiting on [X] from client"
Or "Got Burger just hit [milestone]"
Keeps people invested in the ongoing story.

### "DM me [KEYWORD]" story
Simple text on black:
"If you own a restaurant in Michigan, DM me RESTAURANT"
"If you've been thinking about getting a website built, DM me QUOTE"

### Repost engagement story
When someone DMs a keyword or responds to a story, screenshot it (blur their name).
Post: "This is why we do what we do"
Social proof without being braggy.

---

## Story Schedule (Monday–Sunday)

**Monday** — "What's on the board this week" (overview story)
**Tuesday** — Behind-the-scenes of one thing you're building
**Wednesday** — Price/service reveal (poll or question sticker)
**Thursday** — Client update or progress shot
**Friday** — "Ask me anything" + one inspirational/business story
**Saturday** — Repost or reshare Got Burger content. Show the work lived somewhere.
**Sunday** — "What we shipped this week" recap

---

## Story Highlight Covers

Create 5 highlights. Every Reel goes into the right one.

**GOT BURGER** — All Got Burger content
**AI DEMOS** — AI receptionist calls, demos
**AUTOMATION** — Scrapers, finance system, bots
**SERVICES** — Pricing cards, what we offer
**RESULTS** — Money made, deals closed, client outcomes

## High-Engagement Story Ideas

### "Which would you pick?" Poll Stories
These get huge engagement and warm up the algorithm.

Story 1: "Which website would you trust more?"
→ Show a bad restaurant website vs Got Burger's
→ Poll: "Left" / "Right"

Story 2: "What would you pay for 24/7 AI phone answering?"
→ Poll: "Under $100/mo" / "$150–300/mo"

Story 3: "Does your restaurant need a website?"
→ Poll: "Yes, badly" / "We're fine"

Story 4: "How do you currently take online orders?"
→ Multiple choice: DoorDash / Own website / Phone only / Don't take orders

### "Drop your business below" link sticker stories
"Restaurant owner? Drop your location below — we'll run a free audit."
This fills your DMs with warm leads.

### Countdown sticker stories
"Got Burger going live in [X days]"
Build anticipation for the launch.
