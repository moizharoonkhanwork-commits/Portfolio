## Quick Recording Tips

- Screen record at 1080p minimum
- Use a clean browser — no bookmarks bar, no extensions visible
- Dark mode browser if possible (matches the site's dark theme)
- Add music in CapCut or Instagram before posting
- For music: chill trap or lo-fi instrumental — nothing with lyrics
- Keep your mouse movements slow and intentional
- Caption text: white, clean sans-serif font, large enough to read in feed
