# VIRAL MECHANICS — HOW TO ACTUALLY GET THE VIEWS

## 1. Post the AI demo call first
Do not post anything else until you post the AI receptionist demo.
That is your highest-leverage first impression. Everything else builds on that.

## 2. The 3D burger is your thumbnail/cover image
Use a frame of the 3D spinning burger as your profile cover, highlight thumbnail,
and link-in-bio image. It's your most visually striking asset.

## 3. Create comment bait
End every video with a question or a "comment X to see Y" CTA.
- "Comment DEMO and I'll show you the AI call"
- "Comment AUDIT and I'll run this on your website"
- "Drop your city below — who should we build next?"

## 4. DM the local community
Send the Got Burger video to:
- Muslim community pages in Michigan (it's a halal burger spot)
- Ypsilanti/Ann Arbor local Facebook groups
- Michigan restaurant owners on Instagram
One reshare from a local page = thousands of local eyeballs.

## 5. Tag Got Burger in every post about Got Burger
If Got Burger has Instagram, tag them. They'll repost to their own audience.
Their followers are exactly your target client.

## 6. Use the price as a hook, not a secret
"$1,625 restaurant website" is a specific number that makes people stop.
Always include the price. It builds trust and filters your audience.

## 7. Consistency > Perfection
Post every day for 30 days. Screen recordings are fine.
Low production is fine. Posting nothing because you're editing is not fine.

## 8. Cross-post everything
Same video. Instagram Reels + TikTok + LinkedIn. 
Three platforms, one piece of content, 10x the reach.
